About these references. Free, self-study notes covering the KIIP Korean curriculum.

  • Made for studying. Tap any unit to open it, search vocabulary instantly, and jump between units from the floating bar.
  • Four languages, one Korean. Read in English, Uzbek, Russian, or Indonesian — switching keeps your place in the lesson, and the Korean is identical across all four.
  • Works offline. Every page is fully self-contained and prints cleanly to PDF (Ctrl/Cmd + P).
  • Scope. Levels 1–4 follow the current KIIP textbooks — vocabulary lists are expanded thematic references, not verbatim transcriptions. Level 5 covers the Understanding Korean Society course.
